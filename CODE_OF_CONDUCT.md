# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The RollScript community is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and constructive environment for everyone who participates in the project.

We welcome contributions from people with different backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives.

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

* Be respectful and considerate of others.
* Communicate clearly and constructively.
* Focus on ideas, code, and technical discussions rather than individuals.
* Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
* Respect different opinions and approaches.
* Help maintain a friendly and professional community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

* Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* Threats or intimidation.
* Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
* Publishing private or confidential information without permission.
* Unwelcome sexual attention or other inappropriate conduct.
* Any other behavior that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional and open-source community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

Depending on the circumstances, actions may include:

* A request to stop the behavior.
* A warning.
* Removal of comments or other contributions.
* Temporary or permanent restriction from participating in the project.

Maintainers will make reasonable efforts to handle reports fairly and respectfully.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers through the contact information provided in the repository or organization profile.

Please do not use public issues or discussions to report sensitive or private matters.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

* Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Code reviews
* Other official RollScript community spaces

Thank you for helping make RollScript a welcoming and constructive open-source project.
